Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about online English test.

What is the EF SET?

The EF SET is a free, online English test that assesses your English skills across 3 levels: basic comprehension, grammar, and conjugation.

How does the EF SET English quick test work?

The test adapts to your level and provides accurate results aligned with the CEFR framework.

What are A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 and C2?

These are the six levels of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR).

Is EF SET a good English test?

Yes, it's recognized internationally and used by thousands of organizations worldwide.
